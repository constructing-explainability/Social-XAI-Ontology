# Social XAI Ontology

The Social XAI Ontology is a BFO-based reference ontology that provides a formal foundation for Social XAI. First, the Social XAI Ontology models the entities derived from the co-construction paradigm as a foundation for Social XAI, including explanandum, explanans, understanding, explanation, explainer, and explainee. Second, it presents an ontology of explanatory moves, which serves as the basis for the analysis of explanatory interaction frameworks. Third, it models three dimensions of explanatory progress—adaptivity, understanding, and context—along with their related entities within social explanations in Social XAI, thereby offering deeper insight into how explanation processes evolve.
